FAQs

1. How Do I Submit a Ticket?

  • In Order to submit a ticket you must create an account. Once your account is created you will be able to submit as many tickets as your heart desires. Please keep in mind we are IT people and not Robots, so it may take some time to fix. If your problem involves hardware please bring it to the IT office in Room 404 Building A or Room 420 Building B. If the device is too large to move please report the area and the asset tag of device for our team to find.

2. Have you tried turning it off and on again?

  • This seriously works like 60% of the time! Power down, and then unplug your computer and count to 30. Once done please plug it in and turn it on

3. How do I send a screen shot?

  • If you are using a windows device please press the windows key+shift+s and drag the snipping square over what you wish to show us. Then paste (Ctrl+v) it into an email or document for us. If you have dual screens in 4k HD and screen cap that, we will not be able to access the file because it is so large, and please stop bragging.
